Hello world! I am currently a Solution Architect at Cognizant based in Teaneck, New Jersey, US. My work focuses on designing and implementing innovative technology solutions, with a special emphasis on AI-driven systems, IoT frameworks, and intelligent network security.

I hold a Bachelor of Engineering degree in Information Technology from Dr. Bhim Rao Ambedkar University, Agra, India. Throughout my career spanning over two decades, I have worked with leading organizations including Tata Consultancy Services, CMT Limited, Texport Industries, and Coincap System and Services.

My research interests include deep learning applications in healthcare monitoring, 5G network security using neural learning, and ethical AI in hyper-automation. I am an active member of the academic community, serving as a Senior Member of IEEE ComSoc, Professional Member of ACM, and Fellow of IETE (Institution of Electronics and Telecommunication Engineers).
