I am a seasoned technology professional and researcher with over two decades of experience in designing, architecting, and delivering large-scale, secure, and intelligent digital solutions. Currently, I serve as a Solution Architect at Cognizant in Teaneck, New Jersey, where my work focuses on AI-driven systems, IoT-enabled frameworks, and advanced network and data security architectures that bridge enterprise-scale deployments with cutting-edge research innovation.

I hold a Bachelor of Engineering in Information Technology from Dr. Bhim Rao Ambedkar University, Agra, India, and have built a diverse global career with organizations such as Tata Consultancy Services, CMT Limited, Texport Industries, and Coincap System and Services. As a Software Engineering Specialist, I have made significant contributions to business clients through end-to-end software architecture design, development, and integration across complex domains.

My technical expertise spans Artificial Intelligence, Data Engineering and Data Science, and robust data security frameworks. I have demonstrated strong proficiency in AI/ML/DL-based integration development, implementing resilient security architectures to mitigate cyber threats, and designing advanced software systems for human and animal healthcare, pharmaceutical drug sampling and clinical trial management, and banking platforms.
