About Me

Biography

I am a Solution Architect at Cognizant with over 20 years of experience in the technology industry. My career has spanned across multiple leading organizations, where I have contributed to developing innovative solutions in artificial intelligence, IoT, network security, and automation systems.

Throughout my professional journey, I have focused on bridging the gap between cutting-edge research and practical industry applications. My work involves designing scalable architectures, implementing AI-driven solutions, and ensuring ethical compliance in automated systems. I am particularly passionate about leveraging deep learning for healthcare applications and developing intelligent frameworks for next-generation network security.

As an active member of the research community, I contribute to advancing knowledge in AI, machine learning, and intelligent systems through conference publications and collaborative research projects.
